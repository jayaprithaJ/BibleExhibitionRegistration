## Tips for Best Results

### QR Code Size:
- **Minimum:** 1 inch x 1 inch
- **Recommended:** 2-3 inches x 2-3 inches
- **For large posters:** 4-5 inches x 4-5 inches

### Placement:
- ✅ Bottom right corner
- ✅ Center of invitation
- ✅ Back of invitation card
- ❌ Don't place on folds or creases

### Testing:
1. Print a test copy
2. Scan with multiple phones
3. Ensure it opens your website
4. Check from different distances

### Colors:
- ✅ Dark QR code on light background (best)
- ✅ Black on white (most reliable)
- ⚠️ Colored QR codes work but test thoroughly
- ❌ Light colors on light background (won't scan)

## Shortening Your URL (Optional)

If your URL is too long for printing, use a URL shortener:

1. Go to https://bitly.com/ or https://tinyurl.com/
2. Paste your long URL
3. Get a short URL like: `bit.ly/bible-expo-2026`
4. Use this short URL in the QR code
5. Also print it below QR code for manual entry

## Printing Recommendations

### For Professional Printing:
- Format: PDF or PNG
- Resolution: 300 DPI minimum
- Color Mode: CMYK for print
- Size: Actual size (no scaling)

### For Home Printing:
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Resolution: 150-300 DPI
- Color Mode: RGB
- Test print before bulk printing
